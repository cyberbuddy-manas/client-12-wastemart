About Us

About Us

Welcome to our website, where we are dedicated to providing sustainable and eco-friendly solutions for feeding cattle. At our company, we believe in utilizing food waste to its fullest potential, and that's why we have developed a unique process for producing high-quality cattle feed using waste food. Our process involves collecting food waste from grocery stores, restaurants, and other food-related businesses and then processing it into a nutritious and balanced feed mixture for cattle.
Our mission is to reduce food waste, lower greenhouse gas emissions, and provide a sustainable and affordable feed solution for farmers and ranchers. We believe that by using food waste in this way, we can not only help the environment but also support the agricultural industry by providing a cost-effective feed option for farmers and ranchers.

How we produce Cattle feed with Waste food?

1) One way is to collect food waste from grocery stores, restaurants, and other food-related businesses, and then process it into a feed mixture for cattle.

2) Another way is to use food by-products, such as spent grain from breweries or distilleries, and mix it with other ingredients to make a complete feed for cattle.

3) You can also use food waste from your own kitchen, such as vegetable scraps and fruit peels, and add them to a compost pile. Once the compost is ready, it can be used to feed cattle.

4)Another way is to use food waste such as food processing, and mix it with other ingredients to make a complete feed for cattle.

Food waste is bad for multiple reasons, both environmentally and economically. Firstly, it contributes to greenhouse gas emissions as food that ends up in landfills releases methane, a potent greenhouse gas, as it decomposes. Secondly, food waste is a waste of valuable resources such as water, energy, land, and labor used in growing, producing, and transporting the food. Thirdly, it leads to economic loss for both consumers who waste food and the supply chain participants who grow, produce and sell the food. Lastly, food waste exacerbates global food insecurity by taking away from the food supply that could feed people in need.

Our Mission & Goal

Our Mission at Wastemart is to provide sustainable and eco-friendly solutions for feeding cattle by utilizing food waste to its fullest potential. We believe that by using food waste to produce cattle feed, we can not only help the environment but also support the agricultural industry by providing a cost-effective feed option for farmers and ranchers.

FIRST

Reduce food waste by repurposing it into a nutritious and balanced feed mixture for cattle. Lower greenhouse gas emissions by reducing the amount of food waste that ends up in landfills. Continuously innovate and improve our production process to minimize waste and maximize the use of food waste.

SECOND

Provide a sustainable and affordable feed solution for farmers and ranchers, by using food waste to produce cattle feed at a lower cost than traditional feed source. Continuously improve the nutritional value and safety of our feed by working closely with veterinarians and nutritionists to ensure that our feed is safe and nutritionally balanced for the animals.

Third

Increase the awareness of our sustainable feed production method and educate the public on the importance of reducing food waste. Expand our production capacity and reach more farmers and ranchers to provide them with sustainable feed options.
